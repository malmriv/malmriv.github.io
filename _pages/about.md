Hello! My name is Manuel, and I am based in Granada, Spain. I work in integration, the discipline of building the middleware that allows different systems to communicate with each other.

My background is in physics. I started out studying chemistry before switching degrees, having decided that physics was a better fit for the kind of stuff I was interested in. Physics required a fair amount of programming, which suited my interests well; computational methods and simulation were among the areas I enjoyed most during my degree.

Outside of work, I read a lot, mostly European literature and anything related to Slavic history, culture and politics. I also watch a lot of films; my taste tends towards the contemplative and slow-paced over the action-heavy, though I will watch pretty much anything. I enjoy cooking, particularly vegan food and baking, and have a growing interest in coffee and espresso preparation.

In the [Blog](https://malmriv.github.io/year-archive/) section you will find technical notes and writeups, mostly related to software and integration. My CV is available [here](https://malmriv.github.io/cv/).

For any inquiries, feel free to reach me [via email](mailto:malmriv@gmail.com) or on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/malmriv/).
